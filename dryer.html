AquaCondense Pro

The Innovative Clothes Dryer for Compact Spaces

Key Features

  • Compact Design: Fits into spaces ≤2 sq. ft.
  • Energy Efficiency: Consumes ≤1 kWh per cycle.
  • Safety: Equipped with fire-resistant materials and fail-safe systems.
  • User-Friendly: Adjustable settings and OLED display.
  • Eco-Friendly: Built with recyclable materials.

How It Works

The AquaCondense Pro uses advanced dehumidification technology to extract moisture from clothes. It features a PTC heater for temperature control, thermoelectric Peltier modules for cooling, and sensors to monitor humidity and moisture levels. Real-time updates are displayed on an OLED screen, ensuring precise and efficient drying.

Mechanism & Components

Component Description
PTC Heater Provides heating and dehumidification.
Thermoelectric Peltier Modules Handles cooling and moisture removal.
ESP32 Microcontroller Manages sensors, display, and connectivity.
Humidity Sensors Monitors drying environment.
OLED Display Shows drying progress and settings.
